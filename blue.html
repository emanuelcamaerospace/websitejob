BLUE – Micro Turbojet Project

As part of Aerotéc, I am currently leading the design and optimization of radial impeller and diffuser components for a student-developed micro turbojet engine where I have:

  • Created detailed 3D CAD models of impeller and diffuser components in SolidWorks and Siemens NX, integrating aerodynamic efficiency with manufacturing constraints and structural integrity considerations.
  • Developed an in-house CAD automation process involving Siemens NX and MATLAB, greatly improving design efficiency and consistency.
  • Created tutorials regarding the CAD automation process and its applications, instructing other team members on its use as the new default methodology.
  • Developed MATLAB scripts to calculate blade geometry, flow paths, pressure ratios, and performance parameters for the centrifugal compressor.
  • Conducted CFD simulations in Star-CCM+ to validate impeller performance, analyze flow separation, and optimize blade angles to meet design targets.
  • Ensured system-level integration of the compressor with combustion chamber and turbine sections to balance overall engine performance.
