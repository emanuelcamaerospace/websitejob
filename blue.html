BLUE – Micro Turbojet Project

As part of Aerotéc, I am currently designing radial impeller and diffuser components for a micro turbojet engine. My responsibilities include:

  • MATLAB modeling of turbomachinery performance
  • CAD design of compressor and diffuser components
  • CFD simulation of compressor and diffuser components
  • Integrating aerodynamics principles with manufacturing constraints for production
