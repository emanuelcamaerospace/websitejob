About

It has always been a dream of mine to fly. Initially, I aimed to become an airline pilot, a passion I still maintain through flight simulation and immersion in the aviation industry.

This curiosity led me to pursue Aerospace Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico, Portugal’s most competitive engineering track. Graduating top of my high school class (19.13/20), I moved to Lisbon to pursue my degree.

During my internship at ANAC (Autoridade Nacional de Aviação Civil) in 2024, I developed software capable of predicting the flight path of UAS after critical failures, supporting certification procedures—a contribution recognized by the project coordinator.

I also joined BLUE, a student project focused on micro turbojet design, to gain practical experience in propulsion and aircraft systems, complementing my MSc studies. An Erasmus semester at the Technical University of Munich further deepened my expertise in propulsion and aircraft systems.
